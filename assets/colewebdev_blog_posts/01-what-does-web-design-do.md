## What Does Web Design Do?

Web design is more than making a website look good—it’s about creating a digital experience that turns visitors into customers.

## What Web Design Includes
- Strategic layout
- User experience (UX)
- Local SEO optimization
- Mobile-first design
- Conversion-focused structure

## Why It Matters
A well-designed website builds trust, ranks better on Google, and generates more leads.
